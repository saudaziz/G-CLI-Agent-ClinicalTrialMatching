Clinical Trial Protocol: GLUCO-SAFE-2026
Phase: III
Condition: Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus

Inclusion Criteria (Summary Index):
- Adults aged 18-75.
- Diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes for at least 6 months.
- HbA1c between 7.0% and 9.5%.
- Stable medication regimen for at least 3 months.

Exclusion Criteria / Lab Thresholds (Vector Index):
- Liver Function: ALT or AST > 3x Upper Limit of Normal (ULN). Assume ULN is 40 U/L. (ALT > 120 or AST > 120 is exclusion).
- Renal Function: eGFR < 60 mL/min/1.73m2.
- History of cardiovascular disease (stroke, myocardial infarction).
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding.

Trial Objective:
To evaluate the safety and efficacy of GLUCO-SAFE in patients with Type 2 Diabetes who are not adequately controlled on Metformin.
